ABOUT US

         HotelGuide was founded in 2025 as a group project for the MIS327 Full Stack Development course, with the vision of simplifying hotel search and booking experiences in Istanbul. The founders noticed that most booking platforms were complicated and cluttered. Our goal is to allow users to find hotels clearly without unnecessary steps. We have partnered with several popular hotels, providing up-to-date information on availability, pricing, and reviews. We continuously improve the user interface so even first-time users can easily make reservations. We aim to further expand our services to nearby attractions, maps, and to more hotels in Istanbul and other cities of Turkiye.
